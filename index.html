Who We Are

Bison Components provides engineered steel framing solutions from raw metal components to fully insulated, sheathed panels. With rapid turnaround times, licensed design and engineering support, and organized shipping, we help construction crews build faster, more efficiently, and with consistently high quality.

Why Choose Steel for Your Next Project

  • No warping, shrinking, twisting, or swelling from moisture.
  • Resistant to termites, pests, and mold.
  • Non-combustible and supports fire-resistant construction.
  • Engineered precision for straight walls and clean corners.
  • Consistent strength-to-weight with less material waste.
  • Prefabrication speeds rough-in, inspections, and finishes.
  • Pre-cut service holes simplify electrical and plumbing.
  • Stable supply and pricing reduce project delays.
