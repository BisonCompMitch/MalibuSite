Welcome to Bison Components

Bison Components manufactures full steel framing for homes and businesses. Many commercial buildings specify steel framing. Our machines are fast and our steel supply is overnite so no long waits for components.    

Request an Estimate

Why Steel?

No Dimensional changes. No waste and quick assembly on site or panalized for faster construction. No termites. No mold. We don't wait for wood supply, we don't wait for truss production. Steel is better on price, quality and schedule.

What We Offer

  • Raw steel framing components
  • Assembled steel frame panels
  • Assembled steel frame panels with insulation and sheeting

Tell us what you need and we’ll help you dial it in.

How the Process Works

  • Bring us your plans
  • We analyze for steel framing and provide an estimated cost
  • If we agree, we produce the engineered steel plan
  • With the approved plan, we produce the steel framing components
  • If you need, we assemble the steel framing panels
  • If you want more, we assemble the steel framing panels with insulation and sheeting
  • We provide a transportation bid before proceeding with production
